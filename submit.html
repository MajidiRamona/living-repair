If you know a repair workshop, oral-memory project, foodways collective, or any other living-heritage initiative we should track for COP 31 — recommend it. The form takes about five minutes.

What we're looking for
  • An initiative, business, startup, or organization rooted in living cultural practice — repair, craft, ritual, oral memory, foodways, ecological knowledge.
  • Some concrete climate or sustainability link — emissions avoided, materials diverted, adaptation, resilience, or biodiversity.
  • A real, contactable group — not a one-off event.
