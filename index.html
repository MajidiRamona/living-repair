COP 31 · Intangible Cultural Heritage · 2026

Heritage is no longer fragile. It is a resource.

Songs, repair, rituals, foodways — the living practices our planet has carried for centuries are now also carrying the planet. This platform tracks how intangible cultural heritage is being lost to climate breakdown, and how repair and revival initiatives are quietly outperforming our policy frameworks.
