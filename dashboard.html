The single statistic that matters
Climate-action heritage inscriptions grew from four in 2010 to eighty-one in 2024.
That is not a forecast or a target. It is the count of UNESCO Convention inscriptions that explicitly cite climate adaptation or mitigation as their reason for being. The institution caught up to the planet faster than the policy frameworks built to fix it.
